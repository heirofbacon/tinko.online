Live Interactive Stream Game

Drop balls.
Dominate the stream.

Tinko is a physics-based, fully interactive drop game that runs directly on your stream overlay. Viewers earn currency passively, drop balls, battle each other, and trigger epic on-screen alerts—completely controlling the action.

Standout Features

Everything you need to turn lurkers into active participants.

Duels & Battle Royales

Viewers can challenge each other to high-stakes 1v1 duels, or moderators can open massive Battle Royale lobbies where the winner takes the entire pot.

Custom Avatars & Balls

Let viewers express themselves! They can drop balls that look like their profile picture, specific hex colors, Twitch emotes, or custom uploaded server assets.

Integrated Bot

Tinko natively reads chat and replies to your viewers with their balances, leaderboard ranks, and duel invitations.

TikTok-Style Win Alerts

When a viewer hits a high multiplier, massive animated alerts and sound effects trigger on-stream, giving them the ultimate dopamine rush.

Cross-Platform Support

Whether you stream on Twitch or Kick, Tinko pools all chats together. A viewer on Kick can duel a viewer on Twitch in real-time, seamlessly.
