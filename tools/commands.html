Chat Commands

Everything your viewers and mods need to control the game.

Viewer Commands

!drop <amount> [color|emote|@user]

Drops balls onto the pegboard. Optionally specify a color, "me" for your profile pic, or tag someone to drop their avatar.

> !drop 5 red
> !drop 2 🥓
> !drop 5 @heirofbacon
!color <hex|color|emote|@user>

Permanently change what your balls look like so you don't have to specify it every time you drop.

> !color #ff00ff
> !color 🥓
!balls or !points

Tinkobot will reply with your current ball inventory. You can also tag someone to check their balance.

!leaderboard [@user]

Shows the top 3 players currently on the server, as well as your personal rank.

!duel <@user> <wager>

Challenge another viewer to a 1v1 drop battle. Winner takes the whole pot!

> !duel @chatuser 50
!accept or !deny

Accept or decline a pending duel challenge from another viewer (you have 60 seconds).

!join

Join an active Battle Royale lobby if a Moderator has opened one. Entry fee varies.

Moderator & Admin Commands

These commands require Mod or Broadcaster privileges on the respective platform.

!battle <entry_cost>

Opens a Battle Royale lobby for 60 seconds. Viewers type !join to enter. Winner takes all.

!ballsoff or !ballson

Pauses or resumes the Tinko game globally, hiding it from the overlay.

!addballs <amount> <@user>

Injects currency into a specific user's account magically.

!removeballs <amount|all> <@user>

Removes currency. Use "all" to wipe an account to 0.

!dropmax <amount>

Sets the global limit for how many balls a viewer can drop in a single command to prevent stream lag.
