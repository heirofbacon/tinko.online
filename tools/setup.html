OBS Setup Guide

Getting Tinko running on your stream takes less than two minutes. Follow these instructions to add the interactive layer to your broadcast.

1

Get Your Overlay URL

Log into the Tinko Dashboard to retrieve your unique overlay URL. It will look something like this:

https://play.tinko.online/overlay?key=your_secret_key
2

Add Browser Source

Open OBS Studio or Streamlabs. In your Sources panel, click the + button and select Browser. Name it "Tinko Game".

3

Configure Dimensions

Paste your URL into the URL field. Important: Set the Width to 1920 and Height to 1080 to ensure the physics engine aligns properly with your screen.

4

Enable Audio (Crucial)

Scroll down in the Browser Source settings and check the box that says "Control audio via OBS". This ensures the TikTok-style win alerts and sound effects play through your stream audio correctly.
